AI Voicemail Transcription Overview

Yeastar P-Series Software Edition supports AI transcription feature for voicemail messages, which can converts voicemail audio into readable text using the built-in artificial intelligence (AI), allowing users to quickly review voicemail messages without replaying audios.

Requirements and restriction

Requirements
  • Firmware: 83.19.0.x or later
  • Subscription: Enterprise Plan (includes 120 transcription minutes) or Ultimate Plan (includes 240 transcription minutes)
    Note: You can extend transcription minutes by subscribing to the AI Transcription Add-on service (Path: Plan > Add-on Subscription).
Restriction
The built-in AI voicemail transcription can NOT be used simultaneously with the third-party Speech-to-Text (STT) API integration on the PBX system.

Highlights

Instant & accurate voicemail transcription
Deliver accurate voicemail-to-text conversion within seconds, allowing users to easily review and prioritize important messages.
Effortless transcript review & adjustment
Allow users to easily review, edit, and fine-tune the AI-generated transcripts to correct names, clarify information, or add personal notes.
Flexible transcription modes
Support both automatic and manual transcription modes, allowing users to choose whether to transcribe voicemails automatically or only when needed.
Multi-language support
Provide multiple languages for transcribing of group and extension voicemails. Extension users can set their preferred transcription language for personal messages sent to their extension voicemail.

Configuration steps

  1. Enable and Configure AI voicemail transcription feature

    Enable the AI voicemail transcription feature for the system. Set the desired transcription language for group voicemails and specify which extensions are allowed to use the voicemail transcription feature.

    For more information, see Configure AI Voicemail Transcription.

  2. Customize AI voicemail transcription for extensions

    After the AI voicemail transcription feature is enabled, voicemail messages for authorized extensions are automatically transcribed in a preset language by default. You can configure the transcription mode (either automatic or manual) and preferred transcription language for each extension individually to meet their specific needs.

    For more information, see Configure AI Voicemail Transcription for an Extension.

  3. View AI transcription report

    View the total transcription minutes consumed across all voicemails, allowing efficient monitoring and management on the AI transcription usage.

    For more information, see Access Transcription Usage Details Report.
